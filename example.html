CSS-only tooltip on any element via data-tooltip="…". Default placement is top; override with data-placement="bottom", left, or right. Two styles: compact (default, dark pill for short labels) and regular (data-tooltip-style="regular" — white surface, wrapping body text, caret). Reveals on hover and keyboard focus.
